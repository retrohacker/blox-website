With your cloud, files are:
Private
Built with state-of-the-art encryption. Nobody can access the files in your cloud other than you, no matter who they are.
Versioned
your cloud keeps track of how files change overtime, allowing you to view and restore historical copies.
Persistant
Persistant means that your files stay around. With your cloud, files are synced as soon as a backup location is available. This means your cloud is automatically protecting your data against your computer failing.
Independent
With your cloud, your files are independent of the devices that create them. It doesn't matter where you author a file, on your phone or your laptop, as soon as the file is synced to a backup location it will be available across all of your devices.
